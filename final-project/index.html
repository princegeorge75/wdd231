About Us

Details

Information about Kigo Golfing Club.

History

The club was founded by the Rotaract club of Kigo back in 2006. It started with only 8 members and currently today has over 150 active members.

Vision

Our vision for the future of Kigo Golfing Club is to expand to greater heights, producing world class golfers and inspiring the younger generation.

Upcoming Events

Saturday Golf Club Weekly Meeting

Join us every Saturday for our weekly meeting to discuss upcoming events and connect with fellow golf enthusiasts.

Wednesday Night Meet & Greet

A casual gathering for new and existing members. Meet the team and socialize every Wednesday night.

Friday Golf Trainings and Tournaments

Participate in training sessions and tournaments every Friday. All skill levels are welcome!
