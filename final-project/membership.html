Our Membership Classes

At the Kigo Golf Course Club, we value our esteemed customers and thus we have come up with three different classes to which our interested community can buy and become members of our club;

Gold Membership Package

The Gold Membership Package comes with the following benefits:

  • Absolute free entry to all events
  • Dinner with the club president and board each month
  • Free entrance to any desired tournament
The Gold membership costs $2500 per year

Silver Membership Package

The Silver Membership Package comes with the follwing benefits:

  • 60% Discount on all entrance and registration fee to the club activities
  • 2 free entrances to tournaments per month
  • A quartely spa and massage service at the Golf Course Hotel
The Silver membership costs $1800 per year

Bronze Membership Package

The Bronze membership package comes with the following benefits:

  • Free weekly golf trainings
  • 20% Discount on all club charges
  • 1 Free Tournament entry per month
The Bronze membership costs $1200 per year
