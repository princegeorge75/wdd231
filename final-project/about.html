Information about Kigo Golfing Club.

History

The club was founded by the Rotaract club of Kigo back in 2006. It started with only 8 members and currently today has over 150 active members.

Vision

Our vision for the future of Kigo Golfing Club is to expand to greater heights, producing world class golfers and inspiring the younger generation.

The Kigo Golf Course Club is the official club for all Ugandans and foreigners who wish to enjoy a golfing life in Uganda, especially around Kigo area, on the shores of lake Victoria.
The Kigo Golf course is located in Kigo, Kajjansi town along the Entebbe - Munyonyo Express Highway. Our main offices are also found on the Golf course area.
The golf course sits on an area of 8 acres of land inclusive of some water pads and also the shores of the Lake Victoria.
